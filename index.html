My Supplements

I would like to discuss a little bit about supplements. I get asked a lot what I do as a vegan as far as vitamins go. Now I am not a huge fan of taking a lot of supplements and prefer to try to get the vitamins I need through natural food sources but there are few items I do take.

I do feel we as a society tend to look for a quick remedy instead of looking at why we need a supplement in the first place. Most people avoid looking at the food they eat as something which could be causing the problem. To make matters worse, there are oftentimes other steps you need to take to even get supplements to absorb into your body for use which could render the supplements useless if not done.

Listed below are the supplements I take.
